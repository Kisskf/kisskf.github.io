The Art of Imagination

Art is only a form of imagination. Each form of art is just some type of take on someone's imagination. Every piece of my art comes from a mind filled with dreams and places many things of this world but also many things not of this world. The work of other people is just a reflection of their imagination, and this includes not only physical artwork but books and writings as well.
